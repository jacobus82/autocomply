What makes a follow-up actually work
Most follow-ups fail because of one of these four things.
🎯
One clear CTA only
Every follow-up should ask for exactly one thing. "Are you ready to proceed?" or "Can we chat for 10 minutes?" — not both. Multiple asks create decision paralysis.
📌
Reference something specific
"Following up" is the weakest opener in business. Reference what was discussed, what you sent, or something relevant to their situation. Specific beats generic every time.
⏱
Send at the right time
Tuesday to Thursday, 8am–10am or 1pm–3pm performs best. Avoid Mondays (too busy) and Fridays (already checking out). WhatsApp is read faster than email in SA.
💡
Add a reason to respond
The best follow-ups give the client a new piece of value — a relevant insight, an update, a deadline. "We have one installation slot left in May" is a reason. "Just checking in" is not.
