Hashtag reach tiers explained
Bigger is not always better. For SA businesses, niche and medium tags often drive more relevant traffic than massive ones.
🌍Massive
100M+ posts
Extreme competition. SA audience is a tiny fraction. Use 1–2 max for discoverability. Never rely on them.
🔵Large
10M–100M posts
High reach, mostly global audience. Good for brand awareness but low SA-specific conversion. Use 2–3.
🟢Medium
1M–10M posts
The sweet spot for most SA businesses. Good reach, better audience quality. Use 4–6 in your mix.
🟡Niche
100K–1M posts
SA-specific tags often sit here. Lower reach but high audience relevance — people actively looking for SA content.
🔴Micro
Under 100K posts
Hyper-local or niche. Very small reach but extremely targeted. Good for local events, community, specific towns.
