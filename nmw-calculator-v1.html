Key NMW facts for employers
📅
Effective date
R30.23/hour applies from 1 March 2026. Previous rate was R28.79 — a 5.0% increase.
⚖️
Not negotiable
The NMW cannot be varied by contract, collective agreement, or any other law. It is the absolute floor.
🚫
Tips don't count
Tips, bonuses, and gratuities may not be counted toward the NMW. Employers must pay at least R30.23 before tips.
🏠
Accommodation deduction
Live-in domestic workers: employer may deduct a maximum of 10% of the wage for accommodation provided.
💰
Penalty for underpaying
Employers who pay below NMW face fines of up to twice the amount underpaid under the NMW Act s.73A.
📞
Report underpayment
Call 0800 030 007 (toll-free) or visit your nearest Labour Centre. Reports can be made anonymously.
