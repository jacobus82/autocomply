What works on each platform for SA businesses
Platform conventions differ significantly — the same message needs a different treatment on each.
LinkedIn
Hook in line 1 — people see only the first ~2 lines before "see more". Make it a question, bold statement or surprising fact. 3–5 hashtags max. Stories outperform company updates. South African business, economy and leadership content performs well locally.
Facebook
Short wins — posts under 80 characters get 66% more engagement. Lead with an image or video and use caption sparingly. Facebook groups outperform pages for SA SME reach. Local community angles resonate strongly.
Instagram
Caption isn't the hero — the image/video is. Keep the first line strong then expand. 15–20 niche hashtags placed at the bottom or in comments. SA hashtags like #SouthAfricanBusiness and industry-specific tags (#JoziFood, #CapeTownEats) build local reach.
WhatsApp Status
Direct and personal wins over broadcast-style. Treat it like a message to a friend, not an advertisement. No hashtags. Pair with a strong image. Status disappears in 24 hours — time-sensitive offers work well here.
